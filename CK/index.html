Presence is a profound and transformative concept that extends far beyond mere physical proximity. It encompasses the entirety of our being in any given space, often transcending the visible. Though its effects may be difficult to quantify, their impact is undeniable, fostering an environment where each individual feels uniquely valued and cherished. As a coach, I embody this holistic presence, aligning with the ICF's 8 Coaching Competencies, to create a space of safety and trust. This nurturing presence facilitates healing, growth, and the embracing of one's vulnerabilities, ultimately transforming them into strengths. Through coaching, I invite clients to fully immerse themselves in their journey, much like a sunflower follows the sun, guiding them towards their inner light and potential.

OUR SERVICES

We offer our services online, in person, and even in a hybrid format to accommodate all your needs.

Personal Leadership Coaching

Personal Leadership Coaching involves a dedicated process where a coach partners with an individual to enhance their leadership skills, self-awareness, and effectiveness in achieving personal and professional goals. It focuses on developing leadership qualities, decision-making abilities, and fostering a growth mindset tailored to the individual's unique aspirations and challenges.

Group Leadership Coaching

Group Leadership Coaching is a collaborative coaching process aimed at developing leadership skills within a group or team context. It focuses on enhancing communication, collaboration, and collective problem-solving abilities among team members, fostering a supportive environment for mutual growth and achieving collective goals.

Organizational Development

Organizational Development (OD) encompasses planned, systematic efforts to enhance an organization's effectiveness and resilience. It involves implementing strategies and interventions to improve organizational culture, structure, processes, and systems, aiming to facilitate positive change, growth, and adaptation to internal and external challenges.

Leadership Mentorship

Leadership mentorship involves a structured relationship where an experienced leader guides and supports a less experienced individual in developing their leadership skills, knowledge, and career growth. It focuses on providing personalized advice, insights, and opportunities for the mentee to learn and grow professionally under the mentor's guidance.

Training in Coaching, Mentoring, and Counselling

Training in Coaching, Mentoring, and Counselling equips individuals with the skills and knowledge to effectively guide and support others. This training covers essential techniques and best practices to foster personal and professional development, enhance communication, and build strong, supportive relationships.

Counselling Services

Counselling Services provide individuals with a supportive and confidential environment to explore personal issues, develop coping strategies, and enhance emotional well-being. Professional counsellors use therapeutic techniques to help clients navigate life challenges, improve mental health, and achieve personal growth.

Public Speaking, Conferences, Workshop Moderation

Public Speaking, Conferences, and Workshop Moderation services aim to enhance individuals' abilities to effectively communicate, engage, and inspire audiences. These services provide training and support for delivering impactful presentations, facilitating productive discussions, and leading successful events.

Training on the Power of Journal Keeping

Training on the Power of Journal Keeping emphasizes the benefits of regular journaling for personal reflection, emotional well-being, and goal setting. Participants learn techniques for effective journaling, fostering self-awareness, creativity, and a deeper understanding of their thoughts and experiences.

ABOUT ME

Catherine Wanjohi

I am an executive, team, and life coach with over 10 years of experience in corporate, nonprofit, and individual leadership coaching. Since 2004, I have been the CEO of Life Bloom Services International (LBSI), a nonprofit organization I co-founded, leading it to build strategic partnerships worldwide.

LBSI focuses on working with marginalized populations to promote inclusivity and participation. As a professional coach, I have engaged with various organizations’ staff and boards locally and globally, fostering governance and inclusivity. My leadership style is anchored in Reflective Models for transformation.

I am a Senior Certified Professional Coach with Coach Transformative Academy (Dubai), an Associate Certified Coach (ACC) with the International Coaching Federation (ICF), and a Certified Professional Mentor with the International Mentoring Centre. Additionally, I am a Professional Counsellor with the Kenya Psychologists and Counsellors Association (KPCA).

I am also a member of the International Coach Federation (ICF), based in New York. Based in the Global South, I offer a unique perspective that strengthens existing coaching practices. I specialize in an eclectic approach to coaching. My top three styles are holistic for life coaching, democratic for teams, and visionary for all engagements.

My style is rooted in reflective experiential processes that help clients and myself tap into inner strength, becoming effective in desired situations. I thrive working with individuals, groups, and teams eager to step into their best selves, especially during disruptive moments.

I focus primarily on middle management levels in corporations and organizations, helping bridge the gap between senior management strategies and their implementation at lower management levels.

Additionally, I am an Ambassador with Safaricom Foundation World of Difference 2013 and a Commonwealth Foundation Peer Mentor for Civil Society on Inclusion and Participation.
